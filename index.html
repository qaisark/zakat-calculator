How this calculator computes Zakat

This calculator follows a straightforward educational method and does not store your data. It computes your payable Zakat as follows:

  1. Add all entered assets to get Total Assets.
  2. Add all entered liabilities due now to get Total Liabilities.
  3. Compute Net Zakatable Amount as: max(Total Assets - Total Liabilities, 0).
  4. Compute Nisab Threshold from selected basis: 87.48 × gold price/gram or 612.36 × silver price/gram.
  5. If Net Zakatable Amount is greater than or equal to Nisab Threshold, Zakat is due.
  6. When due, Zakat Payable is calculated as: Net Zakatable Amount × 0.025 (2.5%).

Important: Scholarly differences exist on details (for example, gold vs silver Nisab basis, treatment of specific assets, and timing over one lunar year). For personal rulings, consult a qualified scholar.
